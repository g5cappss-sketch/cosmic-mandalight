GUIDE

How To Play
🎮 CONTROLS
  • Move Mouse to control light.
  • Press 'T' for Light Show Cinematic! ✨
  • Collect Orbs to score.
  • BOSS DIMENSION: Collect 10 scattered nodes while avoiding the Boss chasing you!
🌿 CHILL RELAX
  • No time limit. Save Level. Just relax.
⏱️ TIME RUSH
  • 60s Timer. Avoid Black Holes.
  • Boss Fight: At Lv 5, 10, 15... you enter Boss Dimension.
  • Loot Beams: Catch falling beams for items (Magnet, Freeze, Bomb).
  • Bomb: Destroys 1 Black Hole OR gives +500 Pts.
